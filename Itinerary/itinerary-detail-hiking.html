Mountain Hiking Picnic

Embark on an unforgettable mountain adventure with scenic trails, breathtaking peaks, and serene picnic spots. Perfect for nature enthusiasts and photography lovers.

📍 Margalla Hills / Nathia Gali ⏱ Full Day

Experience Overview

Discover the beauty of Pakistan's mountain trails with our expertly guided hiking adventure. From gentle forest paths to challenging peak ascents, our itineraries cater to all fitness levels with stunning natural scenery and wildlife encounters.

Our mountain hiking experience includes professional guides, gourmet picnic lunches, photography sessions, and optional camping extensions. Explore pristine trails, capture breathtaking views, and create lasting memories in the heart of nature.
